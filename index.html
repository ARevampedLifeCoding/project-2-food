About Us

We are a company specializing in getting the food you want in a timely manner. We also believe that this process should be streamlined for you. All the restaurants you want to eat at in one convenient place. If you want International house of pancakes but your wife wants curry chicken, we have got you covered. One website to bring you all the food you could dream of. During the time of Covid, we know that having someone deliver your food does not sound like fun. The less people touching your food the better. So, once you order through our sight the company you chose will start making the food for each member of your family. Then you will get text message updates letting you know when each of your dishes are ready for pickup. And just like that you have ordered a meal for the whole family!
